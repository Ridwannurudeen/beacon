How Atlas works

  1. 1

    Deposit bUSD

    You deposit Beacon USD into AtlasVault on X Layer testnet. You receive ATLS shares pro-rata to NAV. Your shares track the live equity of the entire agent pool.

  2. 2

    Three agents compete

    Fear chases momentum. Greed mean-reverts. Skeptic consults the Beacon signal layer before every trade. They share an AMM, run on a 30-second tick, and trade against each other's price impact.

  3. 3

    Intelligence costs real money

    Skeptic pays Beacon signals via x402 before each trade. The cost comes out of its book. If the signals don't generate alpha, Skeptic loses to Fear and Greed. Cascade payments aren't theater — they're a tradeoff.

  4. 4

    Performance compounds, on-chain

    Every trade, every signal call, every PnL delta is recorded on AgentRegistry. The leaderboard is live, public, and will outlast the hackathon. Reputation accrues to the strategies that actually work.
